WebGL Gallery

A collection of different projects and experiments.

  • Twist and Tessallation: Twist shapes by recursively subdividing them, and rotating each of the smaller shapes based on their position from the center.
  • Painting with the mouse: Draw lines with different width and color using the mouse.
  • A simple CAD application: With support for adding some basic shapes, and performing some simple transformations on them.
  • A simple CAD with lighting: Same as above, but with some additional functionality for adding colors, shading and lighting.
  • Simple texture mapping: Texture mapping generated patterns, images and videos onto flat and curved surfaces.
